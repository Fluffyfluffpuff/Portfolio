Project Overview

Mindmate AI is a collaborative virtual therapy platform developed in 2023 to bridge the gap between mental health professionals and those seeking support.

The platform acts as a "Collaborative Wellness Navigator," utilizing AI Decision Trees to intelligently triage user needs and facilitate precise matching with the right practitioners.

By combining a user-centric interface with data-driven routing, Mindmate AI ensures that users receive personalized care pathways while providing mental health experts with a streamlined way to connect with the clients they are best equipped to help.
