Project Overview

CPrint is a focused Python-based color detection application developed in 2024 to provide automated identification and labeling of colors within digital images.

By processing uploaded files, the system extracts precise color data and maps it to a predefined library of color names.

This tool offers a straightforward digital solution for users who need to quickly translate visual information into readable color classifications, ensuring consistency in how colors are identified and documented across different projects.
