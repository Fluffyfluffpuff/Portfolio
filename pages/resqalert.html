Project Overview

ResQ Alert is a dedicated mobile emergency response app created in 2025 to improve public safety and coordination in Barangay Bariri, San Jose, Antique.

The application acts as a direct digital connection between residents and local emergency services, replacing traditional and slower methods of reporting with an efficient mobile platform.

By utilizing real-time communication and location-based technology, ResQ Alert aims to greatly decrease response times and offer a more organized system for handling local emergencies, accidents, and medical situations.
