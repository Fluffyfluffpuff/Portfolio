Project Overview

Verdure is a dedicated online knowledge platform established in 2025 to digitally capture and consolidate the rich heritage of traditional herbal remedies and home practices in Antique.

By developing a systematic digital archive, the initiative aims to connect ancient local knowledge with contemporary accessibility, ensuring that native healing traditions are safeguarded for future generations.

Verdure functions as a cultural and medicinal repository, offering a trustworthy space for users to discover the botanical variety and longstanding wellness practices distinctive to the province.
